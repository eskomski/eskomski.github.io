Software developer, WWU Mathematics Department
2016 – 2018

Under the advisement of Dr. Richard Gardner, professor of mathematics at Western Washington University.

Developed and maintained geometrictomography.com, a website summarizing research in geometric tomography, an area of mathematics related to the derivation of information about a geometric object from its sections or projections. This included implementing reconstruction algorithms that demonstrate various concepts and theories in geometric tomography. Tasks included building in-browser visualization tools using D3.js and WebGL, rewriting the backend of the site using Flask, building a RESTful API to facilitate request and retrieval of reconstruction results, and frontend development using jQuery.
