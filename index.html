Past Work

Geometric Tomography

Under the advisement of Dr. Richard Gardner, professor of mathematics at Western Washington University.

Assisting with the development and maintenance of geometrictomography.com, a website summarizing research in geometric tomography, an area of mathematics related to the derivation of information about a geometric object from its sections or projections. This includes implementing reconstruction algorithms that demonstrate various concepts and theories in geometric tomography.
